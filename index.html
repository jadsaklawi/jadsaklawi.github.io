1.3. Decks

  • A Deck fits with an ordered list of labels of Players.
    • Labels are symbols of the Players (Flag, Coat of Arms, Logo, et cetera).
  • A Deck can be of different types:
    • Sovereign Entities
    • Non-state actors
    • Companies
    • Foundations
    • Sub-state actors
      • Agencies
      • Militaries
      • Departments
      • Legislative
